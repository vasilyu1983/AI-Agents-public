## Sidereal vs Tropical Zodiac

The fundamental difference between Western and Vedic astrology:

```
TROPICAL ZODIAC (Western):
- 0° Aries = Vernal Equinox (March 20-21)
- Based on seasons, Earth's relationship to Sun
- Moves with precession

SIDEREAL ZODIAC (Vedic):
- 0° Aries = Fixed star position
- Based on actual constellation positions
- Corrected by Ayanamsa

AYANAMSA = Tropical Position - Sidereal Position
Current Ayanamsa (2025): ~24°11' (Lahiri)

Example:
Sun at 15° Aries (Tropical) = Sun at ~21° Pisces (Sidereal)
```

## Vimshottari Dasha (Primary System)

The 120-year planetary period system:

| Dasha | Duration | Total Years |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| Ketu | 7 years | 0-7 |
| Venus | 20 years | 7-27 |
| Sun | 6 years | 27-33 |
| Moon | 10 years | 33-43 |
| Mars | 7 years | 43-50 |
| Rahu | 18 years | 50-68 |
| Jupiter | 16 years | 68-84 |
| Saturn | 19 years | 84-103 |
| Mercury | 17 years | 103-120 |

**Starting Point**: Determined by Moon's Nakshatra at birth.

## Aspects (Drishti) - Different from Western

Vedic aspects are different:

| Planet | Aspects Houses |
|--------|----------------|
| All planets | 7th from position |
| Mars | Also 4th and 8th |
| Jupiter | Also 5th and 9th |
| Saturn | Also 3rd and 10th |
| Rahu/Ketu | Also 5th and 9th |

**Example**: Mars in 1st house aspects 4th, 7th, and 8th houses.
